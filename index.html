WHAT WE DO

STAND OUT FROM THE PACK

Want more exposure for your band? Need a way to tell the world what makes your business different? Maybe you just want more likes on Facebook? Whatever your needs, Rat Cave Productions can help put you in the spotlight. Here are the services that we offer:

VIDEO

From pre-production to post, we've got the know-how to make your next video project exceptional.

AUDIO

We can record your band, mix the tracks, and then master the record. What more do you want?

PHOTO

For professional photos of your band, your staff, or any moments you might want captured.

FILM

If you feel like going retro with your project, we've got you covered. We think film is pretty neat too.

NEED SOMETHING ELSE?

We can do just about anything you might need to help market, brand, or otherwise promote your identity. If your needs don't fit into the above categories, feel free to drop us a line anyway! Chances are we'll still be able to help you out.
